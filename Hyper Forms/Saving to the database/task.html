Description

It's time to admit new people to our club! Our visitors need a link to fill out our form. Also, let's show the list of the current participants on the main page.

Objectives

First thing's first, you need to create a button or a link leading to the form page on your index page. Now since the introduction form is ready, we don't need the Coming soon line anymore. Let's replace it with a Click here to join! button.

Since we need to store the data on our members in the database, let's create a Participant model in models.py. It will have the same three fields as our previous form: name, age, and favorite_book. A participant should fill out the form and send a POST request. After that, you need to save the received data as a Participant object. You don't need to print it.

Let's come back to index.html, let's add the Current club participants: counter below the join invitation link. At this step, you need to show the singed members of your club. Construct a table that will show the name, age and a favorite book of all our members. If there are no participants, display the following line: No participants so far. Be the first!
